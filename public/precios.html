Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need TopstepX to use Pattro?
No. You can log trades manually in under 10 seconds. The Chrome extension gives you automatic capture for TopstepX users — but the full analytics work with manual entries too.
What happens if I cancel?
Your subscription stays active until the end of the billing period. After that, you lose access to the app but your data is preserved. You can resubscribe anytime.
Is my trading data private?
Yes. Your trades, emotions, and scores are encrypted and never shared or sold. We use Supabase with row-level security — only you can access your data.
What's the difference between Mental Score and Mind Score?
Mental Score is the overall composite (Discipline 40% + Mind 35% + Execution 25%). Mind Score measures your psychological state — emotions, behavior, and color day. They're related but different dimensions.
