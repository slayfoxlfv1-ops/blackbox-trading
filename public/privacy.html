1. Who We Are

Pattro is a trading psychology journal and AI coaching platform available at pattro.com. We are the data controller for the personal information you provide when using our service.

For any privacy-related questions, contact us at [email protected].

2. What Data We Collect

We collect the following categories of information:

Account & Registration Data

When you create an account, we collect: your legal first and last name, email address, username, country, street address, city, zip code, and state/region. This information is required to create your account and comply with our payment processor's requirements.

Trading & Journal Data

All trading data you enter into Pattro — including trades, P&L, emotions, discipline tags, checklist responses, mood check-ins, and personal notes — is stored securely and used exclusively to generate your analysis, scores, and coaching insights.

Usage Data

We may collect basic usage information such as login timestamps, feature usage patterns, and error logs to improve the platform. This data is not linked to your trading content.

Payment Data

Payments are processed by Paddle, our Merchant of Record. Pattro does not store your credit card number, CVV, or other payment credentials. We only receive subscription status and payment confirmation from Paddle.

Support Messages

If you contact support, your message, email, and any information you provide is stored to help us respond to your inquiry.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We share data only with the following trusted service providers, strictly as necessary to operate Pattro:

  • Supabase — database and authentication provider (data stored in the EU/US)
  • Paddle — payment processing and subscription management (Merchant of Record)
  • Resend — transactional email delivery
  • Vercel — hosting and serverless infrastructure
  • Anthropic — AI model powering the Coach IA feature (only your trading data summaries are sent; no personal identifiers)

All service providers are contractually bound to use your data only to provide their services to us and are prohibited from using it for their own purposes.

We may disclose data if required by law, court order, or to protect the rights and safety of Pattro and its users.

5. Cookies & Tracking

Pattro uses essential cookies to maintain your login session. These cookies are necessary for the service to function and cannot be disabled.

We do not use advertising cookies, tracking pixels, or third-party behavioral tracking tools. We do not participate in any ad network.

You can clear cookies through your browser settings at any time, which will log you out of your session.

6. Data Retention

We retain your account and trading data for as long as your account is active. If you cancel your subscription or delete your account, we will retain your data for up to 90 days before permanent deletion, in case you wish to reactivate.

You may request immediate deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by contacting [email protected].

Payment records may be retained longer as required by applicable tax and financial regulations.

7. Data Security

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your data, including:

  • All data transmitted over HTTPS (TLS encryption)
  • Passwords are hashed and never stored in plain text
  • Database access is restricted via Row Level Security (RLS) — each user can only access their own data
  • API keys and secrets are stored as environment variables, never in source code

No system is completely secure. If you discover a security vulnerability, please report it to [email protected] immediately.

8. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access — request a copy of the data we hold about you
  • Correction — request correction of inaccurate data
  • Deletion — request deletion of your account and all associated data
  • Portability — request your trading data in a machine-readable format
  • Opt-out — unsubscribe from marketing emails at any time

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at [email protected]. We will respond within 30 days.

9. Children

Pattro is not directed at children under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal data from minors. If you believe a minor has created an account, please contact us immediately.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will notify you via email or through the Pattro dashboard before the changes take effect.

Your continued use of Pattro after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
