Refund Policy

Last updated: March 2026

We offer a 7-day full refund — no questions asked. If Pattro isn't right for you, just email us within 7 days of your first payment.

7-Day Money Back Guarantee

If you're not satisfied with Pattro for any reason, contact us within 7 days of your initial purchase and we'll issue a full refund to your original payment method.

No forms, no hoops. Just send an email to support@pattro.com with the subject line "Refund Request" and your account email.

After 7 Days

After the 7-day window, we do not offer refunds for the current billing period. You can cancel your subscription at any time and you will not be charged for future periods.

When you cancel, your access continues until the end of the current billing period.

How to Cancel

You can cancel your subscription at any time from your account settings, or by emailing support@pattro.com. Cancellations take effect at the end of the current billing cycle.

How to Request a Refund

Email us at support@pattro.com with:

Subject: Refund Request

Include: Your account email address and the reason for your refund (optional but helpful).

We process refunds within 5–10 business days. The refund will appear on your original payment method.

Exceptions

We reserve the right to deny refunds in cases of abuse (e.g., repeated refund requests across multiple accounts).

Contact

Questions about this policy? Email us at support@pattro.com.
